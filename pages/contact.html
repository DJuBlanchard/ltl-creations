Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

We typically ship orders within 2-3 business days. Delivery times vary by location, ranging from 3-7 business days domestically and 7-14 days internationally.

Do you accept international orders?

Yes! We ship worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times will be calculated at checkout.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact us for a return authorization and refund instructions.

Are your products official Minecraft merchandise?

Our products are Minecraft-inspired fan creations. We're not affiliated with or endorsed by Mojang Studios/Microsoft, but we love celebrating Minecraft culture through quality merchandise.

Do you do custom orders?

Yes! We offer custom orders for bulk purchases and special requests. Please contact us for details and pricing.
